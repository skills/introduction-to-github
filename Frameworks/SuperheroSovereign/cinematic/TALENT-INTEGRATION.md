## VI. Risk Mitigation

### A. Volatility Protection

**Token Price Hedging**:
- Guaranteed minimum USD value for compensation
- Collar options on token holdings
- Staged liquidation windows
- Treasury backstop for emergencies

**Example**: Jordan's $3M token allocation guaranteed minimum $300K USD value regardless of token price

### B. Regulatory Compliance

**Securities Law Navigation**:
- Classification as utility token with governance rights
- Reg D private placement for qualified investors
- KYC/AML for all token recipients
- Legal opinions from top entertainment and crypto lawyers

**Tax Optimization**:
- Structure to minimize tax burden
- Expert advisors for all recipients
- Jurisdiction planning
- Charitable donation options

### C. Reputation Management

**If Blockchain Element Fails**:
- Traditional backend payments guaranteed regardless
- Ability to convert tokens to cash at predetermined rate
- No reputational risk for participants
- Standard Hollywood contracts as fallback
