**ScrollVerse Connection**:

**Key Narrative Elements**:
- The Great Frequency Distribution - Superman's power shared across humanity

- Birth of the **Sovereign Frequency Protocol** - blockchain-encoded heroic legacy

**ScrollVerse Connection**:
