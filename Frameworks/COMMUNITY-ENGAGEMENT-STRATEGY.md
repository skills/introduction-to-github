# Community Engagement Strategy
## Building a Thriving, Self-Sustaining ScrollVerse Ecosystem

---

## Executive Summary

The **Community Engagement Strategy** outlines the comprehensive approach to fostering meaningful connections, sustained participation, and collective growth within the ScrollVerse ecosystem. This framework transforms passive users into active ScrollSouls, contributors into leaders, and individuals into a unified, sovereign community.

### Strategic Objectives

1. **Maximize Daily Active Participation**: 50%+ DAU/MAU ratio
2. **Foster Deep Connections**: Average 10+ meaningful interactions per user per week
3. **Enable Creator Economy**: 10,000+ creators earning sustainable income
4. **Build Decentralized Governance**: 80%+ community participation in key decisions
5. **Scale Global Impact**: 1M+ documented transformations and healings

## I. Community Architecture

### A. User Journey Framework

```
Discovery → Registration → Onboarding → Activation → Retention → Advocacy
```

#### 1. **Discovery Phase**
- **Channels**: Social media, influencer partnerships, word-of-mouth, media coverage
- **Goal**: Generate awareness and curiosity
- **Metrics**: Website traffic, social media reach, referral clicks
- **Touchpoints**: Landing pages, video explainers, testimonials

#### 2. **Registration Phase**
- **Experience**: Seamless wallet connection or email signup
- **Goal**: Convert visitors into registered users
- **Metrics**: Signup conversion rate, drop-off points
- **Incentives**: Welcome bonus (100 ScrollCoin), referral rewards

#### 3. **Onboarding Phase**
- **Experience**: ScrollSoul Onboarding Journey (6 modules, 60 min total)
- **Goal**: Educate and activate new users
- **Metrics**: Module completion rate, time to completion, quiz scores
- **Rewards**: 1,000 XP, 500 ScrollCoin, "Awakened Sovereign" badge

#### 4. **Activation Phase**
- **Experience**: First ritual, first NFT, first community interaction
- **Goal**: Establish habitual engagement
- **Metrics**: First action completion rate, day 7 retention
- **Incentives**: Streak bonuses, achievement unlocks, social recognition

#### 5. **Retention Phase**
- **Experience**: Daily rituals, content consumption, community participation
- **Goal**: Maintain ongoing engagement
- **Metrics**: DAU/MAU ratio, session frequency and duration, churn rate
- **Strategies**: Push notifications, email campaigns, new content releases

#### 6. **Advocacy Phase**
- **Experience**: Referrals, content creation, governance participation
- **Goal**: Turn users into evangelists and leaders
- **Metrics**: Referral rate, UGC volume, DAO proposal submissions
- **Recognition**: Ambassador status, speaking opportunities, equity/tokens

## V. Governance & Participation

### A. ScrollVerse DAO

#### 1. **Proposal System**
- **Submission**: Any ScrollSoul with 10,000+ XP or 1,000 SC deposit
- **Categories**: Treasury allocation, protocol upgrades, partnerships, community initiatives
- **Discussion Period**: 7 days for community debate
- **Voting Period**: 5 days for token-weighted voting
- **Quorum**: 10% of eligible voters must participate
- **Execution**: Automated on-chain execution for approved proposals

#### 2. **Voting Power**
- **Sources**: ScrollCoin holdings, NFT ownership, XP level, streak bonuses
- **Calculation**: Weighted formula to prevent plutocracy
- **Delegation**: Option to delegate votes to trusted representatives
- **Transparency**: All votes are public and verifiable on-chain

#### 3. **Council Elections**
- **Frequency**: Annual elections for Proof-of-Healing Council
- **Candidates**: Self-nomination with community endorsements
- **Voting**: One person, one vote (verified by ScrollSoul SBT)
- **Term**: 2-year terms, staggered to ensure continuity
- **Accountability**: Monthly reports, quarterly AMAs, recall mechanisms

---

### B. Community Moderation

#### 1. **Volunteer Moderator Program**
- **Eligibility**: 5,000+ XP, positive reputation, application & interview
- **Training**: Comprehensive onboarding on guidelines and tools
- **Responsibilities**: Monitor content, respond to flags, educate users
- **Compensation**: XP bonuses, ScrollCoin stipends, exclusive badges
- **Recognition**: Quarterly awards for outstanding moderators

#### 2. **Conflict Resolution**
- **Process**: Private mediation → Council review → Community vote (if needed)
- **Principles**: Restorative justice, learning opportunities, proportional responses
- **Transparency**: Anonymized case studies shared for community learning
- **Appeals**: All decisions can be appealed to higher governance levels

## VI. Growth & Retention Strategies

### A. Referral & Ambassador Programs

#### 1. **Sovereign Referral System**
- **Mechanics**: Unique referral link for each user
- **Rewards**: 50-100 ScrollCoin per successful referral
- **Bonuses**: 10% of referee's lifetime ScrollCoin earnings
- **Tiers**: Higher rewards for high-volume referrers
- **Recognition**: Leaderboards, exclusive badges, ambassador status

#### 2. **Ambassador Program**
- **Selection**: Application-based, top referrers and contributors
- **Benefits**: Monthly stipend, exclusive swag, direct leadership access
- **Responsibilities**: Content creation, event hosting, community support
- **Training**: Regular workshops and strategy sessions
- **Community**: Private ambassador group for collaboration

---

### B. Retention Tactics

#### 1. **Re-engagement Campaigns**
- **Triggers**: 7, 14, 30 days of inactivity
- **Channels**: Email, push notifications, social media DMs
- **Content**: Missed highlights, exclusive offers, personal messages
- **Incentives**: Return bonuses, limited-time rewards, streaks forgiven

#### 2. **Churn Prevention**
- **Early Warning Signals**: Declining engagement, negative sentiment, feature abandonment
- **Intervention**: Personalized outreach, surveys, support resources
- **Win-back Offers**: Bonus ScrollCoin, free premium access, exclusive content
