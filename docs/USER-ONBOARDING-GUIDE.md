**Q: Where can I buy $MIRROR?**

A: No minimum for holding tokens. Staking requires 1 MIRROR minimum.

A: Yes, all on-chain benefits transfer with the NFT.

**Q: Can I unstake early?**
A: Flexible stakes: Yes. Locked stakes: Only with penalty.

**Q: How often are rewards distributed?**
A: Rewards accrue continuously and can be claimed anytime.

**Q: What happens if I add more tokens to existing stake?**
A: Additional tokens are added; lock period remains for original stake.

### Governance

**Q: How do I vote?**
A: Delegate your tokens (even to yourself), then vote on active proposals.

**Q: What can the DAO change?**
A: Treasury allocation, protocol parameters, partnerships, and upgrades.

**Q: Can proposals be cancelled?**
A: Only by the proposer or admin before execution.
