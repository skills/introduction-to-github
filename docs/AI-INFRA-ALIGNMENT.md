### TPUv7 Ironwood Alignment

The new TPUv7 Ironwood brings significant advantages for ScrollVerse workloads:

- **256x256 Systolic Arrays**: 4x larger than v5, ideal for Codex matrix operations
- **HBM3e Memory**: Higher bandwidth for merkle tree traversal
- **ICI Scale-Up Networking**: Multi-pod scaling matches ScrollVerse's multi-chain architecture
- **RAS Focus**: Higher uptime = more consistent yield generation
