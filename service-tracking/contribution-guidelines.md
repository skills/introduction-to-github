## What Counts as a Contribution?

### 1. Content Creation
**Description**: Creating educational, inspirational, or informative content  
**Examples**:
- Blog posts and articles
- Video tutorials and talks
- Podcast episodes
- Artwork and NFT designs
- Infographics and visual guides
- Music and audio transmissions

**Base Impact Score**: 10-100 points  
**Quality Multipliers**: 1.0x - 2.0x  

**How to Record**:
```json

  "details": {

    "contentType": "blog_post",
```

## Quality Assessment Criteria

### Content Quality (1.0x - 2.0x)
- **Exceptional (2.0x)**: Original, deeply insightful, professional quality
- **High (1.5x)**: Well-researched, valuable, clear presentation
- **Good (1.2x)**: Helpful, accurate, adequate presentation
- **Standard (1.0x)**: Basic, functional, meets minimum requirements

### Impact Factors
1. **Reach**: How many people benefit
2. **Depth**: How profound the impact
3. **Originality**: Uniqueness of contribution
4. **Quality**: Excellence of execution
5. **Timeliness**: Relevance and urgency

## Verification Process

### Automatic Verification
Some contributions are verified automatically:
- On-chain activities (voting, transactions)
- GitHub commits and PRs
- Timestamped content uploads

### Manual Verification
Other contributions require verification:
- Healing sessions (self-reported + community validation)
- Subjective quality assessments
- High-impact claims

### Verification Stages
1. **Submission**: Lightworker submits contribution
2. **Initial Review**: Automated checks (24 hours)
3. **Community Validation**: Optional peer endorsement (48 hours)
4. **Final Verification**: Verifier approval (72 hours)
5. **Reward Distribution**: Automatic upon verification

## Best Practices

### DO ✅
- **Be Honest**: Accurately represent your contributions
- **Be Specific**: Provide clear details and evidence
- **Be Consistent**: Regular contributions build reputation
- **Be Supportive**: Endorse others' quality work
- **Be Patient**: Verification takes time

### DON'T ❌
- **Don't Inflate**: Exaggerating diminishes trust
- **Don't Spam**: Quality over quantity
- **Don't Plagiarize**: Original work only
- **Don't Game**: System manipulation is detectable
- **Don't Forget**: Record contributions promptly

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Register
```bash
# Register as a lightworker
POST /api/v1/service-tracking/register
{
  "address": "0x...",
  "displayName": "YourName",
  "bio": "Brief introduction"
}
```

### Step 2: Record Your First Contribution
Use the API or web interface to submit your contribution details.

### Step 3: Wait for Verification
Check status at `/api/v1/service-tracking/status/{contributionId}`

### Step 4: Receive Rewards
Rewards are automatically distributed to your wallet upon verification.
