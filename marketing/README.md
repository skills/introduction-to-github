# Sovereign TV Marketing Roadmap

## Overview
Strategic marketing plan to attract Founding Supporters for Sovereign TV and FlameDNA NFT holders.

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Awareness)

### Objectives
- Build anticipation for Sovereign TV and FlameDNA NFT launch
- Grow email list and Discord community
- Generate social media buzz

### Channels
- Twitter/X: Daily posts, thread campaigns
- Discord: Community building, exclusive previews
- Telegram: Announcement channel
- Instagram: Visual content, Stories, Reels
- YouTube: Teaser videos, creator interviews

## Phase 2: Launch (Acquisition)

### Founding Supporter Campaign
- Early bird pricing for first 1,000 subscribers
- Genesis NFT airdrops for subscribers
- Exclusive Discord roles and access

### FlameDNA NFT Launch
- Allowlist registration
- Genesis tier presale
- Public mint

## Phase 3: Growth (Retention & Expansion)

### Content Marketing
- Weekly Sovereign TV exclusive releases
- Monthly NFT holder events
- Quarterly community updates

### Referral Program
- Refer a friend, earn ScrollCoin
- Tiered rewards for top referrers
- Community ambassador program
