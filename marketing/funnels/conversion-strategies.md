# Conversion Funnel Strategies

## Overview

Strategic funnels for attracting Founding Supporters to Sovereign TV and FlameDNA NFT holders.

---

## Funnel 1: Founding Supporter Acquisition

### Stage 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)
**Traffic Sources:**
- Organic social media (Twitter, Instagram, TikTok)
- Paid social ads (targeted interest-based)
- Influencer partnerships
- Content marketing (YouTube, Medium)
- SEO-optimized landing pages

**Content:**
- Problem-aware content: "Why mainstream platforms fail creators"
- Solution-aware content: "The sovereign alternative to Big Tech"
- Teaser videos showcasing content quality
- Creator testimonials

**Metrics:**
- Impressions
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)

---

### Stage 2: Interest (Middle of Funnel)
**Touchpoints:**
- Landing page visit
- Email capture (lead magnet)
- Discord join
- Free tier signup

**Lead Magnets:**
- "The ScrollVerse Guide" (PDF)
- Free 7-day trial of premium content
- Exclusive video preview
- 100 ScrollCoin welcome bonus

**Nurture Sequence:**
```
Day 0: Welcome + Guide delivery
Day 2: Platform tour video
Day 4: Community highlights
Day 6: Founding Supporter benefits
Day 8: Limited-time offer
```

**Metrics:**
- Email open rate
- Click rate
- Discord join rate
- Free trial conversions

---

### Stage 3: Consideration (Decision Point)
**Content:**
- Tier comparison page
- ROI calculator (ScrollCoin value)
- FAQ page
- Live demo/webinar
- Case studies

**Objection Handling:**
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | Show annual discount (2 months free) |
| "What if I don't like it?" | 30-day money-back guarantee |
| "I don't understand crypto" | No crypto required for basic membership |
| "Is this legitimate?" | Show team, roadmap, existing community |

**Metrics:**
- Time on pricing page
- FAQ engagement
- Demo attendance
- Support tickets

---

### Stage 4: Conversion (Bottom of Funnel)
**Triggers:**
- Limited-time discount
- Scarcity messaging (X spots left)
- Social proof (X members joined today)
- Personal outreach for high-intent leads

**Checkout Optimization:**
- Clear tier comparison at checkout
- Trust badges (secure payment, guarantee)
- Multiple payment options (card, PayPal, crypto)
- Order bump: Annual subscription (save 2 months)

**Post-Purchase:**
- Immediate access confirmation
- Welcome email sequence
- Onboarding checklist
- Community introduction

**Metrics:**
- Conversion rate
- Average order value (AOV)
- Cart abandonment rate
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)

## Funnel 2: FlameDNA NFT Holder Acquisition

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Traffic Sources:**
- Crypto Twitter (CT)
- NFT-focused Discord servers
- NFT calendars (Icy.tools, NFTCalendar)
- Web3 influencers
- Scroll ecosystem communities

**Content:**
- Utility-first messaging
- Art previews (drip campaign)
- Roadmap reveals
- Team AMAs

---

### Stage 2: Allowlist Registration
**Requirements:**
- Discord join + verification
- Twitter follow + retweet
- Email signup
- Referral bonus entries

**Allowlist Tiers:**
```
OG List (First 500):
- Guaranteed Genesis mint
- 20% discount
- Exclusive trait

Founding List (500-2000):
- Priority Genesis mint
- 10% discount

Public Allowlist (2000+):
- First access to public mint
```

**Engagement Activities:**
- Daily quests for entries
- Art contests
- Meme competitions
- Community contributions

---

### Stage 3: Pre-Mint Hype
**Timeline: 7 days before mint**

**Day 7:** Full collection reveal
**Day 5:** Utility breakdown video
**Day 3:** Smart contract audit results
**Day 2:** Final allowlist confirmation
**Day 1:** Last-minute FOMO content
**Mint Day:** Live Twitter Space, Discord event

**Content:**
- Countdown timers everywhere
- Influencer endorsements
- Price + supply confirmation
- Mint instructions tutorial

---

### Stage 4: Mint Execution

1. Genesis Allowlist (2 hours)
2. Founding Allowlist (4 hours)

**Communication:**

## Funnel 3: Cross-Sell Flow

### Sovereign TV → FlameDNA
**Trigger:** Active subscriber for 30+ days

**Sequence:**
1. "As a Sovereign TV member, you get priority access to FlameDNA"
2. Show exclusive holder benefits (additional SCR, higher discounts)
3. Limited-time holder discount for subscribers
4. Testimonials from subscribers who became holders

---

### FlameDNA → Sovereign TV
**Trigger:** NFT purchase or wallet connection

**Sequence:**
1. "Maximize your FlameDNA utility"
2. Show tier benefits unlocked by holding
3. Free 1-month Genesis tier trial
4. ScrollCoin bonus for subscribing

---

## Traffic from Sovereign TV Platform

### Integrated Promotion
**Locations:**
- Homepage banner for NFT allowlist
- Content end cards promoting subscription upgrade
- Wallet connection prompt with holder benefits
- In-app notifications for limited offers

### Connected Wallet Flow
```
1. User connects wallet on Sovereign TV
2. Check for FlameDNA NFT ownership
3. If holder: Auto-apply benefits, show holder dashboard
4. If not holder: Show "Unlock holder benefits" CTA
5. Track wallet for future airdrops and promotions
```
