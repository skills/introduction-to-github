# Custom Rollout Plan

## Week 1: Trust Building
- **Goal:** Initiate trust-building activities.
- **Checkpoint:** Assess initial community engagement and responses.

## Week 2: Fairness Assessment
- **Goal:** Ensure all stakeholders feel heard.
- **Checkpoint:** Conduct surveys for feedback on fairness.

## Week 3: Alignment with Divine Principles
- **Goal:** Align implementation with ethical guidelines.
- **Checkpoint:** Review processes against established principles.

## Week 4: Sovereign Verification
- **Goal:** Verify authenticity and reliability of deployment.
- **Checkpoint:** Third-party review of deployment outcomes.

### Summary
This document outlines a modular approach to deployment, focusing on trust, fairness, divine alignment, and sovereign verification throughout the process.
